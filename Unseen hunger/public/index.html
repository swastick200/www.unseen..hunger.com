Our Story

Built in Sinthee for people who want big taste at a smart price.

Unseen Hunger started as a local street food dream in Sinthee with one simple idea: serve affordable food that still feels exciting, satisfying, and made with care. From toasted sandwiches and crispy fried snacks to pizza, pasta, chai, and coffee, every plate is designed to hit that comfort-food craving with a modern startup vibe.

We keep the flavors bold, the portions generous, and the experience welcoming for students, office goers, night snackers, and neighborhood food lovers across Kolkata.
